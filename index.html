Quick Margin Check (Python)


revenue = 1250_000_000
net_income = 150_000_000
net_margin = net_income / revenue * 100
print(f"Net margin: {net_margin:.2f}%")
Use to illustrate computational reproducibility and sanity checks outside the BI tool.

Financial Formulas

Net Profit Margin: \[ \text{NPM} = \frac{\text{Net Income}}{\text{Revenue}} \times 100 \]

Compound Annual Growth Rate (CAGR): \[ \text{CAGR} = \left(\frac{V_f}{V_i}\right)^{\frac{1}{n}} - 1 \]

Call out NPM as key profitability metric; use CAGR for multi-year growth storytelling.
